🦦 Sea Otter

Look for floating groups and kelp beds. Use 5×, Burst, and short 4K60 clips.

🦅 Bald Eagle

Scan treetops and harbor pilings. Expose slightly down against bright sky.

🐋 Humpback Whale

Watch for blows, then aim ahead of movement. Video often beats still photos.

🐳 Orca

Keep the frame wider until direction is predictable. Avoid chasing with digital zoom.

🦭 Harbor Seal

Look near ice and rocky shorelines. Use 5× and steady bracing.

🐻 Bear

Photograph only from safe, guided locations. Keep distance and follow excursion staff.
